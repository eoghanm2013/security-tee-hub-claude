# Engineering Messages

When writing a message to bring to engineering (bug report, escalation, etc.), use this format:

1. **Title line:** Bold, leads with the user-visible symptom
2. **One paragraph:** What happens, why it happens (code path), and where in the codebase the issue is
3. **One line:** Who reproduced it and customer impact
4. **One line:** Suggested fix if known
5. **Refs at the bottom:** SCRS ticket, GitHub links

No headers, no section dividers, no bullet-point breakdowns. Just a tight paragraph that respects the reader's time and assumes they're technical. Keep it short.
